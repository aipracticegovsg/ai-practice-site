## Philosophy

We believe in:

- **Specialization with Baseline**: Deep expertise in specific domains while maintaining essential foundational skills
- **Practical Over Theoretical**: Focus on live problem-solving rather than rote memorization
- **Continuous Adaptation**: Frameworks designed to evolve with the rapidly changing AI landscape
